Causelist Management System
Purpose: Guide complainants to the correct courtroom within the Rera MP office building by electronically displaying causelists (lists of applicants and respondents) on screens at entry doors.
Key Features:
  • Internal web application: Runs securely within the office building, not accessible from the public internet.
  • Real-time causelist updates: Multiple authorized users from each courtroom can log in and enter causelist data, ensuring up-to-date information for complainants.
  • Improved efficiency: Reduces confusion and saves time for complainants by directing them to the right courtroom directly.
  • Technology stack: Back-end developed with PHP/MySQL for robust data management, and front-end built with JavaScript for a user-friendly interface.
Benefits :
  • Enhanced transparency: Streamlines the court process for complainants with clear and readily available information.
  • Increased convenience: Eliminates the need to search for courtroom assignments, leading to a smoother experience.
  • Reduced administrative workload: Automates causelist updates, freeing up courtroom personnel for other tasks.
